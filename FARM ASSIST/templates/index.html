Seasonal Crops

Summer Crops

Tomato: Thrives in warm weather and is a staple in Indian cuisine. Requires well-drained soil.

Watermelon: Sweet and refreshing. Ideal for hot climates with sandy loam soil.

Corn: Requires full sunlight and is a major crop in India during summer.

Winter Crops

Carrot: Grows best in cool climates. Needs deep, loose soil for optimal growth.

Spinach: A leafy vegetable rich in iron. Thrives in the winter season.

Cauliflower: Prefers cool weather and is a popular winter crop.

Rainy Crops

Rice: Thrives in waterlogged fields. A staple food in India.

Sugarcane: Requires a tropical climate and is a key crop for sugar production.

Soybean: Grows well in rainy seasons and is a significant source of protein.
